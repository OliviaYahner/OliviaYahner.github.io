Welcome to the Cognitive Digital Accessibility Project

This Digital Accessibility project focuses on cognitive disabilities, specifically users with reading disorders like dyslexia and hyperlexia. Dyslexia is a reading disorder characterized by difficulty with word recognition and fluency, often stemming from phonological deficits. Hyperlexia is rare disorder where individuals have advanced reading skills but struggle with comprehension.

The intended audience includes content providers wishing to accommodate users with Dyslexia or Hyperlexia.

This guide does not include specific implementation details for HTML, PDF, Javascript, etc.

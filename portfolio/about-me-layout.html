A little more about me...

In my spare time I enjoy writing Fantasy and Horror stories. Growing up, I kept journals when on vacation and during signifigant events in my life. These are often a source of my writing pool and I review them periodically to pad my fiction with realistic scenarios. I enjoy travelling, on a plane, in a car or on a train.

My Biology minor stems from an avid fascination for animal care. I've kept a menagerie of house pets that include several uncommon species. Some of my favorite pet projects have included

  • Chameleons (veiled, panther)
  • African Pygmy Hedgehog
  • Hairless guinea pigs
  • Madagascar Hissing Cockroach
  • Axolotls

As a compromise for my "hobby" of raising so many animals, each one required a short research paper before taking on the responsibility of bringing them into our home. This directly led me toward my chosen fields of study, as writing and animal care were always something closely entwined.

My most interesting animal experiment included breeding the Axolotls. While I anticipated a yield of a dozen or so, the final (shocking) product was over 200 healthy younglings!
